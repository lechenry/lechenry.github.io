Delfino Studio's Deep Below

About the Project

When two unlikely friends, a scuba diver and an angler fish, get trapped at the bottom of the ocean, they must work together to get back up to the surface. Solve puzzles, avoid enemies, and get to the top before your air supply runs out!

Contributions

This game was created in collaboration with Ava Giozoni, Julia Newberry, and Tess von Rueden. I helped create the title screen, player select screen, credits page, as well as the background art and parallax effects in the tutorial and main levels. I also made the breakable blocks, the anemones, and the treasure cut scene at the end of the tutorial. Lastly, I contributed to level design and created some of the player sprites that were used in the game.

Technology Used

Unity, C#, Photoshop, JIRA

The Legend of Zelda NES (Unity Remaster)

Try the web version here:

WebGL Build

About the Project

This is a remaster of The Eagle, the first dungeon from Nintendo Entertainment System's The Legend of Zelda. There is also a custom level that introduces a new ice weapon that allows the player to freeze water tiles, freeze enemies, and create pushable ice blocks.

Contributions

This game was created in collaboration with Julia Newberry. My responsibilities included 1) Player movement, 2) Room transitions, 3) All of the enemies, 4) Locked doors and keys, 5) Collectibles, and 6) Audio and Sound. I also helped with fixing bugs. For the custom level, I was in charge of creating the new level layout as well as creating the ice tiles and ice blocks along with their features.

Technology Used

Unity, C#, Photoshop, JIRA
